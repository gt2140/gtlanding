About Me

▼

Born in Madrid and raised in Buenos Aires, I've been a crypto-native since my first Bitcoin purchase in 2014. With over 8 years in the blockchain industry, I've lived in 15+ cities, turning a 10-day trip into a 14-month adventure that shaped my global perspective on decentralized innovation.

My background in finance, combined with hands-on experience in P2P exchanges and international crypto transfers, has given me a unique perspective on the intersection of technology and finance.

As a self-taught technologist, I've continuously expanded my knowledge in blockchain, AI, and emerging technologies, always staying ahead of the curve in this rapidly evolving space.
